Jaguar

The jaguar is the 3rd largest of the big cats after the tiger and the lion and it is the largest of all the big cats in the Americas

  • Scientific name:Panthera onca
  • Length: 1.2-2meters
  • Average lifespan: 12-15years
  • Habitat: Tropical Rainforest and Savanas

Elephantidae is the only surviving family of the order Proboscidea; extinct members include the mastodons.
